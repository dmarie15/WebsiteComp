About Us



CatsForYou is an exclusive online pet store which is designed to fit our customers' needs. For our customers' convenience, we have included several different breeds of cats with some basic information, so that you can get a feel for each of the breeds. We also provide a cat shop, which sells quality products for your feline friends, ranging from cat food to scratching posts, collars to squeaky toys. All of these items are accessible by a click of a button; quick and simple. Whether you need medical help, standard items or pampering for your cat, we've got you covered.
