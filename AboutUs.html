About Us


CatsForYou is an exclusive online pet store which is designed to fit our customers' needs. For our customers' convenience, we have included several different breeds of cats with some basic information, so that you can get a feel for each of the breeds. We also provide a cat shop, which sells quality products for your feline friends, ranging from cat food to scratching posts, collars to squeaky toys. All of these items are accessible by a click of a button; quick and simple. Whether you need medical help, standard items or pampering for your cat, we've got you covered.



Cats in the news

Scottish wildcat: 'Rarest kittens' in the world rescued, group says 19th July 2018

Wildcat Haven says the animals were found "dehydrated" and "weak" in the West Highlands of Scotland. The group says the kittens are being cared for until they're old enough to be released into the wild. Only 35 wildcats are left in Scotland, Wildcat Haven says. The kittens' rescue offers hope to a species which is near extinction. Read more at here to read more.

Henrietta the kitten was born with two extra legs 31st July 2018

Her extra limbs are impeding her growth so Feline Friends is fundraising to raise money for an operation. She's currently being looked after in Wallsend but hopes to find her forever home after the operation. Watch the video here

Cat stows away in Chatteris windscreen repair van 6th July 2018

Vets are trying to find the owner of a curious cat that stowed away in the back of a windscreen repair van. The mobile technician discovered the ginger and white female when he returned to his home in Chatteris, Cambridgeshire, after working across East Anglia. The unchipped cat could have hopped in the back from as far away as Ipswich, vets said. Appeals have been put on social media to locate her owner. Read more here
